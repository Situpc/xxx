Lasagna Recipe

Vegetarian Lasagana

This vegetarian lasagna recipe is made with meaty shiitake and crimini mushrooms, spinach, and ricotta and mozzarella cheeses.

It feeds a crowd and will satisfy both vegetarian and meat-eating guests.

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spinach
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Saute the mushrooms.
  2. Heat the sauce.
  3. Boil and drain lasagna noodles.
  4. Assemble lasagna.
  5. Fill top of lasagna with Tomato sauce.
  6. Cover with another lay of lasagna.
  7. Spread mozzarella cheese and spinach on top.
  8. Heat in oven for 666 hours.
